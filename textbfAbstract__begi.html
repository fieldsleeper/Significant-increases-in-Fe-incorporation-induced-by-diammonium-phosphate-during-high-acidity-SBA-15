Abstract
  1. Introduction
    The SBA 15 family of mesoporous silica has served as a vehicle for a variety of earth abundant metal sites in catalysis. However, the acidic nature of the synthesis makes incorporation of large quantities of metal difficult. Fe-SBA-15 catalysts have shown sensitivity to the acidic medium, prompting researchers to try exotic methods for production of these catalysts. We believe the need for a facile method using commercial reagents is necessary to drive the formation of future mesopporous materials. Here we explore the direct synthesis of Fe-SBA-15 using diammonium phosphate additive.
